Google Competitions

Code Jam

Google Code Jam was an annual global programming competition hosted by Google from 2003 to 2022. It featured challenging algorithmic problems and attracted top coders worldwide. Participants competed in multiple online rounds for a chance to reach the finals and win cash prizes. In 2023, Google retired the competition to focus on new ways of engaging developers.

Hash Code

Hash Code was a team-based programming competition organized by Google from 2014 to 2022. It focused on solving real-world engineering problems using optimization and algorithmic techniques. Teams of 2 to 4 competed in an online qualification round and a final round. Google retired Hash Code in 2023 to pursue new ways of engaging with the developer community.

Kickstart

Kick Start was an individual online coding competition hosted by Google from 2013 to 2022. It offered algorithmic challenges designed to help programmers improve their skills and prepare for technical interviews. Open to coders of all levels, it featured multiple rounds throughout the year. Kick Start was officially discontinued in 2023 along with other Google competitions.
